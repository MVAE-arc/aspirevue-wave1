Engagement Posture — How We Work

For the duration of this engagement, Michael functions as a priority-availability advisor and embedded operator to Larry and the AspireVue team. The working pattern includes a weekly working session, ad-hoc calls and screen-share as the work demands, and team-wide visibility into the operating system as it is built. Communication runs through whatever channel moves the work fastest — voice or async or in person — with no procedural overhead introduced by the engagement itself.

Work product produced inside this engagement (workflows, agents, templates, design system, customer-facing assets) is owned by AspireVue and travels with AspireVue. The proprietary-layer methodology behind the work product (the Arc'kin Endeavors operating frameworks and architectural primitives that produce it) remains with Arc'kin Endeavors and is not transferred. This boundary lets the engagement build at full velocity without ambiguity about what AspireVue owns at the end and what remains available through future working relationships.

Day 00 — Engagement Initiation

From contract execution through Day 1, the engagement enters an initiation phase. Spec capture, template build, and engagement preparation proceed without economic gates — the engagement operates as if already in motion. Day 00 is on the house. The purpose is straightforward: Day 1 begins with momentum already running rather than from a cold start.

The Day 00 deliverable is a continuously-evolving spec brief, co-authored by Michael and Larry, that becomes the source of truth for the 30-day implementation phase. Travel and engagement preparation costs incurred by Arc'kin Endeavors during Day 00 are absorbed by Arc'kin Endeavors and are not billed to AspireVue.

If at any milestone the engagement is not landing, either party exercises the cancellation clause; otherwise, the cadence continues.

On-Site Presence

Michael will maintain on-site presence in the Landrum / Greenville area for the duration of the 60-day engagement, with travel back to New Jersey permitted as schedule allows. Travel expenses are absorbed by Arc'kin Endeavors and are not billed to AspireVue. The specific on-site versus remote working pattern is co-determined week-to-week based on the work in front of us.

What's NOT In This Engagement

The 60-day engagement covers the operating stack (Phases 1–3) and the customer-facing creative and design system (Phases 4–7) as scoped above. Outside this engagement's scope:

  • Ongoing operator or fractional-executive work after Day 60
  • Direct execution as AspireVue (Michael is not a member of the AspireVue team or a fractional officer)
  • Any deliverable beyond the proof-of-concept scope of Phase 5's 3D prototyping step or Phase 6's VC asset suite
  • Anything that bleeds into a post-Day-60 ongoing retainer surface (see “Post-Day-60 Working Relationship” below)

Items in this list are not closed doors. They are simply not inside the five-installment commercial structure of this SOW. Any of them can be added to a separate, mutually-signed agreement when and if the work calls for it.

Risk Reversal & Cancellation

Either party may terminate this engagement after Week 1 by providing written notice to the other party. Any installment covering a period that has not yet commenced is refunded or not invoiced. Work product produced through the termination date remains AspireVue's.

A mutual non-disclosure agreement governing confidentiality in both directions was signed by both parties on April 18, 2026. That agreement remains in effect and is not re-executed by this document.

Post-Day-60 Working Relationship

Post-Day-60 working relationships — ongoing strategic counsel, operator-tier engagements, monthly retainers — are discussed in the final week of the 60-day engagement based on actual outcomes. None are presumed, pre-priced, or required.
