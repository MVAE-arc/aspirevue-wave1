How this works.

Three things to know before you start.

01 — Submit often
Each submission stands on its own.
Nothing overwrites. You can hit a section three times across three days and every version is on my end. The last word isn't locked until Day 1.
02 — I see it the moment you send
No waiting on a check-in.
Every submission lands with me in real time. I'm shaping Day 1 in the background as you go, so by the time I'm in front of you we're already dialed in.
03 — Sloppy is the right format
Half-formed beats nothing.
If you know something needs to happen but can't articulate it yet, send that. A sentence and a hunch is useful. Reading between the lines is the job.
