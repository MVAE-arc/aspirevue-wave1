AspireVue · Day 00

The spec brief.
In your words.

This is where the custom implementation gets built. Not by me writing a deck and asking you to react to it — by you putting your thinking here in pieces, as it comes to you, over the next few days before Day 1 begins.

There is no right format. Sloppy is fine. Incomplete is fine. You can come back to any section as many times as you want — every submission is captured in real time and I see it immediately.

Total time 90–120 min across 10 days
Single sitting Never more than 30 min
Window Now through Day 1 (5/7–5/10)

How this works.

Three things to know before you start.

01 — Submit often
Each submission is its own record.
Nothing overwrites. You can submit the Workflows section three times across three days and every version is kept. The last word isn’t locked in until Day 1.
02 — I see it immediately
Real-time notification every time you hit send.
No waiting for a check-in call. Every submission lands in my inbox within a minute. I’m building the implementation picture in the background as you go.
03 — Sloppy is the right format
Half-formed is better than nothing.
If you know something needs to happen but can’t articulate it yet, submit that. A sentence and a hunch is useful. I’m trained to read between the lines.

The brief.

Five sections. Start wherever feels most alive right now.
